Education and Background

I earned my bachelor's degree in Film and Video from the University of Wisconsin Milwaukee, where I honed my skills in visual storytelling and media production. Following my passion for artistic expression, I pursued a master's degree in Fine Arts from the prestigious School of the Art Institute of Chicago. During my time there, I explored innovative techniques and developed a deep appreciation for the intersection of creativity and technology.


Professional Journey

Throughout my career, I have engaged in a variety of creative endeavors. From directing and producing films that capture the essence of human emotion to experimenting with interactive art installations that challenge conventional boundaries, my work reflects a commitment to pushing artistic and technological limits. My projects range from thought-provoking short films that delve into societal issues to dynamic multimedia installations that invite audiences to engage actively with the artwork. Each endeavor is a testament to my belief in the power of storytelling and the transformative potential of technology.


At the heart of my work lies a dedication to storytelling that resonates on a profound level. Whether through film, interactive media, or emerging technologies, I strive to evoke emotions, provoke thought, and inspire meaningful connections. Each project is an opportunity to explore new narratives, experiment with innovative techniques, and leave a lasting impact on audiences.


Beyond Work

When I'm not immersed in creative pursuits or coding challenges, you can find me exploring art galleries for inspiration, capturing moments through photography, or hiking in nature to recharge. I believe in a holistic approach to creativity, where diverse experiences and perspectives enrich my artistic vision and technical expertise.
